Rupee Number Guessing Game


This is you standard crystal number guessing game, only it is a little different. Instead of using the usual crystals for this game, you will be using Rupees from the popular Legends of Zelda games. For those of you who don't know, Rupees are the currency that is used throughout the Legends of Zelda games and they generally appear in the form of crystals. Wow! Who wouldn't want crystals as money?



The object of this game is to click on one of three differently colored Rupees that will randomly correspond with a number. You will not be able to see the number until you click on one of the Rupees, which will cause the number value assigned to that Rupee to be added to your score. What you are trying to do is to get the total of your score to be equal to the number that is presented above the Rupees. Once you do this you will win the game; otherwise, you will lose the game.
